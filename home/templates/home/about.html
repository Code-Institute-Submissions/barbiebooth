About Me And My Barbie Booth

Hi Barbie Collector,

As a response to an - almost traumatic - event in which I lost my very first Barbie Doll (it was actually the very first doll that was given away to the crowd during a huge Barbie launch event in Amsterdam, 1961), I started collecting all Barbie Dolls that were released between 1964 and 1972.

After years of diligent tracing at collector’s events and markets, my collection was almost complete. I had collected hundreds of authentic Barbie dolls, Skippers, and Kens. All mended, and with their original clothing and outfits. Aside from that, I had a massive collection of clothing and accessories for the dolls.

But after years of enjoying the collection, I feel it’s time to let it go. It’s also an emotional process, so why not do it step by step, in her own online store. I promised her once that if I would be able to, I would build the online store for her. So here I am taking the first step.

The community of Barbie collectors is huge and I would like this webshop to be a popular point for Barbie collectors all over the world. That is why every product needs a detailed description, with the original title of the Barbie/accessory along with its release year, and state of condition.

Some dolls are extremely rare and expensive. Finding an exact copy of it will be hard. So from one Barbie collector to another, I hope you like my webshop and may it support you in completing your own collection.

,Geiske
